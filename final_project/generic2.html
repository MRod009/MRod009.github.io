London

London is a sprawling metropolis that much more than the sum of it's parts. Before arriving there I had some preconceived notions on what the city would be like. Those were quickly thrown out the window becuase the city was far more captivating than I had imagined. It is one of the most culturally diverse cities in the world, with a mix of people from all over the globe. It is busy and fast paced like New York but, with a vibe that is distinctly British. For information on which neighborhoods to visit when in London then visit Best Neighborhoods in London.

I was always told that British food was bland and simple, but this could not be further from the truth. The food scene in this city is one of the best in the world. The sheer range of cuisine in London is mind boggling. You can have French pastries for breakfast, Indian food for lunch, and then authentic Shanghainese cuisine for dinner with a three block radius! It is simply impossible to cover all the food choices in this short blog but here is a great website to get you started 38 places to eat in London.
