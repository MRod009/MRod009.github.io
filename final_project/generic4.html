Seoul

Seoul, Korea is huge and has gained a ton of attention in the past decade for not only a beautiful culture but also the K-Pop wave that has swept over the world. When you begin to plan your trip to the capital city of South Korea it can seem overwhelming and you may not know where to begin. While the city has plenty of off the beaten path spots, many of the tourist attractions in the city are actually worth visiting. Make your time in Seoul, Korea the best it can be by being adventurous and open minded! For more valuable travel information on Seoul please visit The Thrillist guide to Seoul.

Deciding on a place to eat in Seoul can be a daunting task. The city is moving at 1000 mph, the lights are flashing and smells can be new and familiar all at the same time. Not to worry, you are not alone most Western tourist have this feeling when visiting Seoul for the first time.
