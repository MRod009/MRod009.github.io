Paris

What more can be said about the city of light that hasn't already been said? It is a beautiful city with countless world class sights and museums. The Louvre is prehaps the most well known museum in the city. It holds a wide range priceless art works from around the world. The most famous being the Mona Lisa painted by Leonardo da Vinci. I could go on and on about the museums in and around Paris but this page would be too long. For more in formation on museums in paris visit Top Museums in Paris

The cuisine in the city as also one of the things that draws millions of tourist to the city each year. The French are known for their cheese, patsries, and decadent dishes. You can eat for very cheap or outragously expensive, there is something for every pallet and budget in this city. Every neighborhood in the city has their own distinct ethinic group that honor the cuisine of their homeland along with a French flair.
