Crete, Greece

Greece has hundred of islands, each with their own unique culture and aesthetic. Crete is the largest island in this archepelago nation. It has so much to offer, from beautiful beaches to ruins that date back thousands of years. The beach pictures about is Balos beach which is hard to get to but 100% worth the 2.5 km hike it takes to get there. Every civilization that controled the isalnd has left there mark on the Cretean culture. This is a must visit island if you are ever in Greece.
